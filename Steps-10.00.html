 Creating Calendar Table
1. On Power BI desktop under Modeling click on new table button 
2. Type the Dax formula below in the Dax formula bar, click on the check mark, and save
 

Calendar = ADDCOLUMNS (
  CALENDAR(MIN('fortune_global_500_from_2019-20'[Start of year]), MAX('fortune_global_500_from_2019-20'[Start of year])),
  "DateAsInteger", FORMAT([Date], "YYYYMMDD"),
  "Year", YEAR([Date]),
  "Month Number", FORMAT([Date], "MM"),
  "YearMonthNumber", FORMAT([Date], "YYYY/MM/dd"),
  "YearMonthShort", FORMAT([Date], "YYYY/mmm"),
  "MonthNameShort", FORMAT([Date], "mmm"),
  "MonthNameLong", FORMAT([Date], "mmmm"),
  "Quarter", "Q" & FORMAT([Date], "Q")
)
