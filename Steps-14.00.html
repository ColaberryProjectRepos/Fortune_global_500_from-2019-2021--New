Step Name : Adding Title and Logos 14




 To insert the Colaberry logo and the furtune 500 logo follow the following steps: 

1.From the Insert tab , click on the image button and select the 

Colaberry logo and click open.

2. In the format pane, go to general and enter the below properties.

Height=62
Width=251
Horizontal= 18
Vertical =12
Title=off
Effects->Background=on, Transparency=0%
Furune 500 Logo-
1.From the Insert tab , click on the image button and select the Furune 500  logo and click open.
2. In the format pane, go to general and enter the below properties.
Height=88
Width=224
Horizontal = 1055
Vertical =0
Title=off
Effects->Background=on, Transparency=0%

Project Title-

1. From the Insert tab, click on the text box button, click inside the box and enter the title for the project as below

Title= Fortune Global 500 (2019-2021)
Font= Segoe UI, center aligned,
Color= #073562
size=32
2. In the format pane, go to general and enter the below properties.
Height=88
Width=1280
Horizontal = 0
Vertical =0
Title=off
Effects->Background=on, Transparency=0%
